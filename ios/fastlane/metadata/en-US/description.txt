MyHealth is your all-in-one personal health and fitness operating system. Track workouts, monitor vitals, manage diet, and stay on top of your care plan — all in one app.

KEY FEATURES:

CARE TAB
• Connect MyChart via SMART-on-FHIR for read-only access to your medical records
• Snap insurance cards and lab reports with on-device OCR — no data leaves your phone
• Personalized care plans for hypertension, diabetes, heart conditions, and more
• Find doctors by ZIP code and specialty
• Medicine reminders with adherence tracking

DIET TAB
• Calorie, protein, carb, and fat tracking with daily goals
• Water intake tracker with customizable targets
• Barcode scanner for instant food lookup
• Meal photo AI estimation
• Custom meal builder and food diary
• Diet suggestions tailored to your health conditions
• Order from nearby restaurants (Sweetgreen, Mendocino Farms, and more)

TRAIN TAB
• Adaptive workout recommendations based on sleep, HRV, and training load
• Today's plan with warm-up, strength, and cooldown blocks
• Exercise library with 30+ moves and form tips
• Anatomy picker — tap a muscle group to find exercises
• Sedentary alert with standup timer
• Custom workout builder

WORKOUT TAB
• Activity rings (Move, Exercise, Stand) synced with Apple Health
• GPS run/walk tracker with pace and map
• Workout logger for sets, reps, and perceived load
• RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) rating after every session
• Sleep stages, HRV, and recovery score
• Wellness insights correlating cardio, strength, and recovery

PRIVACY & SECURITY
• HIPAA-aligned architecture — PHI encrypted on-device, never synced to cloud without consent
• All OCR (insurance cards, lab reports, nutrition labels) runs 100% on-device
• No health data in crash reports or analytics
• Guest mode — use every feature without creating an account
• Optional cloud sync when you're ready

MyHealth works with Apple Health, HealthKit, and Apple Watch to bring all your health data into one place. Whether you're managing a chronic condition, training for a marathon, or just trying to eat better — MyHealth has you covered.
